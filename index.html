Owls

I've always liked owls. But by accident I came to be associated with owls even more amongst my friends and family.

It started when I spent a summer studying archaeology in Greece with the American School of Classical Studies, the organization which runs the American archaeological excavation in Greece. I wanted to get souvenirs for my friends and family, so since I spent a lot of time in Athens, I figured I'd get them owl souvenirs. The owl is the symbol of Athena, the patron diety of Athens before Christianity came, and thus a symbol for Athens as well. In fact ancient Athenian coins had a depiction of an owl.

However, my friends and family didn't understand this connection and just thought I was obsessed with owls. I've explained it to them since but the connection was already made and its stuck.

I've ran with it ever since.
